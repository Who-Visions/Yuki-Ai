*   **Concept:** Instead of hoping a video generator understands "turn around," generate the keyframes yourself.
*   **Technique:**
    1.  **Start Frame:** Generate your character facing forward.

    3.  **Video Generation:** Feed *both* images into Veo or Kling as "Start Frame" and "End Frame."
    4.  **Result:** The video model simply interpolates the movement between your two perfect images, guaranteeing the ending looks exactly how you want.
