Imagen product recontext on Vertex AI lets you generate images of products in different scenes or backgrounds. You provide an image of a product and a prompt, and Imagen product recontext generates images of your product in new scenes or with different backgrounds.

To request access to Imagen product recontext, complete the Vertex AI - Generative Media for Marketing Access Request form.
