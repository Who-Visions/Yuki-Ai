## Universal Pro Tips

### Fabrics
- **Spandex**: Athletic/fitted (Nobara, Tifa, Ahsoka)
- **Satin/Silk**: Elegant/regal (Daenerys, Elsa, Ganyu)
- **Tulle/Organza**: Ethereal layers

### Props (EVA Foam)
1. Cut from pattern
2. Heat-gun to shape
3. Sand smooth
4. Primer coat
5. Acrylic paint
6. Clear seal

### Frost Effects
- White spray paint ($8-10)
- White fabric tape
- White hair chalk
- Silver texture spray
- White eyeshadow/highlighter

### Timeline (6 Weeks)
- Week 1-2: Buy materials
- Week 2-4: Build/sew base
- Week 4-5: Props, details
- Week 5-6: Wig, makeup practice

### Con Day Kit
- All costume pieces + backups
- Wig + bobby pins + glue
- Full makeup + touch-up kit
- Props + repair tape
- Comfortable walking shoes
- Phone charger, snacks, water
